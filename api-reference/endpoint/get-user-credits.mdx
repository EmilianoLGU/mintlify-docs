## Response Fields

### Credits Information
- **`total`** - Your total monthly credit allocation
- **`used_this_month`** - Number of credits consumed in the current billing period
- **`remaining`** - Credits remaining for the current billing period
- **`usage_percentage`** - Percentage of credits used (0-100)

### Billing Period Information
- **`current_period_start`** - Start date of the current billing period
- **`next_refresh_date`** - When your credits will refresh (next billing cycle)
- **`days_until_refresh`** - Number of days until your next credit refresh

### Account Information
- **`created_at`** - When your account was created
- **`last_updated`** - When the data was last updated

<Note>
  **Credit Refresh**: Credits refresh monthly based on your billing cycle. The exact refresh date depends on when your account was created or when you last upgraded your plan.
</Note>
